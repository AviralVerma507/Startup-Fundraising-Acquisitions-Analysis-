# Startup Fundraising & Acquisitions Analysis 🚀

A comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing the **Crunchbase Startup Dataset**. This project explores global investment trends, market dynamics, and acquisition statuses across thousands of startups.

## 📁 Repository Structure
* **Startup Fundraising & Acquisitions PPower Bi Report**: It is the `.pbix` Power BI file.
* **The Crunchbase Startup Fundraising & Acquisitions Dataset**: It is the raw CSV dataset used for the analysis.
* **Report Images 1 - The Crunchbase Startup Fundraising & Acquisitions Dataset_page-0001 (1) & Report Images 2 - The Crunchbase Startup Fundraising & Acquisitions Dataset_page-0001 (1)**: Visual preview of the dashboard pages.
* **Report PDF - The Crunchbase Startup Fundraising & Acquisitions Dataset/**: PDF version of the full report for quick viewing.

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

### Page 1: Market Insights
Focuses on the geographical distribution and sectoral analysis of startup funding.
* **Interactive Map**: Visualize funding by country and region.
* **Market Breakdown**: A list-based exploration of industry sectors (Software, Biotech, Health, etc.).
* **Funding Trends**: An area chart showing how investment has evolved over time.
* **Status Filtering**: Quick-access buttons to filter by 'Operating', 'Acquired', 'Closed', or 'IPO'.

### Page 2: Deep Dive Analysis
Utilizes advanced AI visuals to understand the "Why" behind the data.
* **Decomposition Tree**: Breaks down total funding from a high-level view (Status) down to specific Markets and Cities.
* **Advanced Slicing**: Synced filters to maintain context across the entire report.

## 📑 Data Analysis Report & Key Insights

Based on the analysis of **49,438 startups**, several critical trends in the fundraising landscape were identified:

#### 1. Global Funding Overview
* **Total Market Investment:** The total funding across all tracked organizations reached **$650.93 Billion**.
* **Dominant Market:** **Biotechnology** emerged as the most heavily funded sector, accounting for approximately **$69.47 Billion** in total investment.
* **High-Growth Sectors:** **Mobile ($47.8B)** and **Software ($39.9B)** follow closely, indicating strong investor preference for tech-heavy industries.

#### 2. Geographical Hotspots
* **US Leadership:** The United States remains the primary hub for startup activity.
* **State-Level Analysis:** Within the US, **California (CA)** is the undisputed leader in fundraising, followed by **New York (NY)** and **Massachusetts (MA)**.

#### 3. Fundraising Lifecycle
* **Round Distribution:** Venture rounds represent a significant portion of the funding pie, indicating a mature ecosystem for scale-ups.
* **Temporal Trends:** There has been an exponential increase in activity starting in the late 1990s, with a significant peak in fundraising occurring around **2014**.

#### 4. Operational Status
* While the majority of startups remain **Operating**, a significant segment has reached the **Acquired** stage, providing successful exits for investors.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Power BI Desktop**: Dashboard creation and visualization.

* **DAX**: Custom measures for total funding and company counts.

3. Open the file to interact with the slicers and visuals.
